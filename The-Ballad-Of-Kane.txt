Chapter xFF Finality this the last ever chapter - The Choir Pit

Beneath the city, below the subway lines and the sewer tunnels, there is another level of Atlanta that does not appear on any map.

Concrete corridors run for miles. Industrial lights buzz overhead. The air smells like antiseptic and iron.

Marcus Kane walks the halls like a conductor entering an orchestra pit.

But the orchestra is not instruments.

It is people.

Rows of steel platforms fill a chamber the size of an aircraft hangar. Hundreds of bodies lie there—wired into machines that measure heartbeat, stress hormones, neural spikes.

They are singers.

Not volunteers.

And tonight, for the first time, the choir is loud enough to make the entire world sing.
